Teachers' Day Celebration at CTU Danao: Honoring Educators

CTU Danao recently celebrated Teachers' Day with a grand event that honored the incredible contributions of educators to the community.

Students and faculty gathered in a heartfelt celebration to show their appreciation for the dedication and hard work of their teachers. The event featured a variety of performances, including musical numbers, dances, and skits, all thoughtfully prepared by the students to honor their mentors. In addition to these performances, several speeches were delivered, highlighting the invaluable role teachers play in guiding and inspiring their students.
